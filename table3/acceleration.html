What Will Acceleration Programme Give You?

►
Embarking on a startup journey? Let us be your guiding light, providing comprehensive support to empower, expand, and excel.

Secure up to HK$4.8 million in milestone-based funding tailored to your needs. As well, consult with a domain specialist who can help you navigate the financial landscape and expand your horizons regionally and globally.

With a network and professional services, the largest startup ecosystem is an excellent foundation for your company to accelerate to the next stage.

Is Acceleration Programme Right for You?

►
If your startup possesses innovative technology, ready-for-market products, and a scalable business model, then the Acceleration Programme is tailor-made for you. Join forces with a passionate team of like-minded individuals who share your drive for success.

How Do You Apply for Acceleration Programme?

►
Ensure you meet all the admission criteria before submitting your application to unlock the path to startup success:
  1. Are a growth-stage company looking to scale globally
  2. Are current incubation company or a tenant of HKSTP
    *if you are not currently based in HKSTPC premises, shall obtain qualification approval for moving into HKSTP premises within 6 months from the date of admission.
  3. Have headquarters, executive functions and a corporate CEO based in Hong Kong
  4. Have less than 100 employees
  5. Have at least 50% of full-time staff members based in Hong Kong engaging in technology-related work
Submit your application form with a company deck to acceleration@hkstp.org.
