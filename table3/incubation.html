Is Incubation Programme Right for You?

►
HKSTP Incubation is the perfect launchpad for startups with proven market potential through MVPs or prototypes. If your business boasts a disruptive application, an innovative business model, and a life-changing product or solution, we invite you to apply.

How Do You Apply for Incubation Programme?

►
Ensure you meet all the admission criteria before submitting your application to unlock the path to startup success:
  1. A Hong Kong tech based limited company, established within 5 years of the programme application submission date
  2. At least 2 full time staff working on site and not less than 50% of the team shall be R&D related
  3. The founding team holds at least 51% of the company shares collectively
  4. 3-year R&D and business plan at HKSTP
  5. The company has a prototype or MVP which is being market validated in principle
