What Will Ideation Programme Give You?

►
Experience the power of pre-seed feeding with a generous HK$100,000 financial grant as you immersive yourself in a collaborative startup community with access to experienced mentors, businesses matching, persuasive pitching course and many more.

Is Ideation Programme Right for You?

►
If you have an innovative idea supported by robust R&D and strategic business planning, the application process is open to all Hong Kong ID holders and companies registered in Hong Kong but less than two years old by the application start date.

Dare to dream, seize the opportunity, and let your entrepreneurial spirit soar to new heights.

How Do You Apply for Ideation Programme?

►
Ensure you meet all the admission criteria before submitting your application to unlock the path to startup success:
  1. Submit your proposal for the assessment on Evaluation Criteria, with reference to a template provided on HKSTP website.
  2. Complete the online application with supporting documents.
Evaluation Criteria
  1. Team competency
  2. Business development potential
  3. Innovativeness
  4. Research & Development
Application Timeline
Ideation Programme is open for application every January, May, and September.
